The 34 participating teams gathered in the Jackson Richardson gymnasium at the Université de Villetaneuse to take part in this iconic event.

Once they had settled in, their equipment out and plugged in, the 24h de l'info got under way with a presentation of some of the organizers before the real thing began... the algorithm test.

Our teams

Ibuprofen Fans

The Ibuprofen Fans is one of two teams from the Villetaneuse IUT.

It is made up of 4 1st year students and one 2nd year student.

Their goal in coming here was to have fun and not finish last.

They managed to accomplish their objective, since they finished 28th out of 34, despite having a laptop that stopped working.

Paracetamol Enjoyers

The Paracetamol Enjoyers is the other team from the Villetaneuse IUT.

It is made up of 5 3rd year students on sandwich courses.

Thanks to their experience obtained during their internships and their good mood, they did a great job and finished 4th overall.

They are happy to be part of this competition.

However, there are teams from IUTs all over France, including one from Martinique and La Rochelle!

In the end, it was the Synazes, the geek timbres, and Long-Fa Esport who finished first, second, and third. They came from Calais, Vannes, and Maubeuge respectively.
