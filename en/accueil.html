Welcome on the website of the 24 heures de l'info 2024 !

History

The '24h de l'info' are national competitions that take place every year at an IUT randomly selected. Every student in 'BUT Informatique' is free to participate.

The 24 hours are split into 3 parts of 8 hours each. The first is about algorithms, the second about web development, and the last one about cybersecurity/cryptography.

Each part of the competition is split in multiple exercises too. Succeeding in one grants you a specific number of points, and the team which has gained the most points at the end of the 24 hours wins.

The event of 2024

Context

This year, our school at Villetaneuse has been chosen to organize the 24 hours competition. From Friday 31st of May to Sunday 1st of June, 34 teams did their best to try to win.

We had the pleasure of organizing the event and its development. Let's go back in detail on how we did it.

The steps

Everything started at the beginning of May, when we have been told that we would be in charge of organizing the event. Our class had been divided into 34 groups of 5 people, and every group must realize many tasks such as creating a route sheet, a diary, and displays.

The day before the event, we have set up the tables of every group that was participating. The day of the event, before 2 p.m., we installed many power outlets in order to supply every group with power for their laptops. Then, we have welcomed them by gifting them stuff, and bringing them to the place where they stayed for the next 24 hours.

During the competition, our groups took turns to stay available for all the participants in case they had questions. We also organized the meals so the participants could eat whenever they want, event during the night. We took advantage of their breaks to collect their feedbacks on the event and its organization. Finally, we took many photos to keep a record of the event.

At the end, some of us stayed during the prizes distribution.
